About

Graduating in November of 2022 Kellyn is an emerging archivist and Music Librarian anxious to apply her education, experience, and energy to a new career! Kellyn brings with her two decades of experience as an international performer on trombone, tuba, and euphonium in all genres of music that inspires people with her warm sound, fierce passion, and dynamic stage presence. As an educator, Kellyn is currently on the faculty of the Federal Way School of Music and online through Lessonface for private music instruction. Kellyn is a sought after arranger of brass and showband music.
